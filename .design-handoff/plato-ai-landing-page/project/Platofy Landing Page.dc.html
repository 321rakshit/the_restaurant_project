Why this matters now.

Indian restaurants operate on 5–12% net margins. The economics of running one are quietly getting worse.

01

Aggregator commissions are 18–28% base, often 25–35% effective after platform fees and ads.

02

Zomato and Swiggy raised their per-order platform fee to ₹17.58 in March 2026 — a 19.2% hike.

03

Restaurants below ₹7.5 cr turnover get no GST input tax credit on the 5% they collect — a permanent margin drag.

04

Aggregators control customer identity. Restaurants can't market to their own guests directly.

We think the answer isn't another billing system. It's a new layer that sits on top of whatever a restaurant uses today and gives them back direct customer relationships, margin, and modern tools.

What we're building.

V1, in development. Shipping to design partners first.

Direct ordering

QR code on the table or in the hotel room. Guest scans, sees your menu, orders, pays via UPI. Order goes straight to your kitchen. No app downloads, no aggregator cuts. Works for dine-in, in-room dining, and resort multi-outlet ordering.

AI menu setup

Photograph your existing paper menu. Our AI extracts the dishes, prices, categories, and veg/non-veg markers — and builds your digital menu in minutes instead of days.

A music feature unlike anything in India

We're working on letting guests queue songs to your speaker system from their phone. Owner sets the catalog and rules. Optional, built for cafes and lounges where ambience matters. Still in design; licensing in progress.

We're not trying to replace Petpooja or Restroworks. Platofy sits on top of whatever billing you use today.

Who we're for.

Cafes and restaurants. Independent operators and small chains who want direct orders, control over guest data, and a way off paper menus — without a multi-year POS contract.

Hotels. Properties that want in-room dining guests will actually use — scan, order, charge to the room or pay by UPI. Works alongside your PMS.

Resorts and multi-outlet properties. One platform across every F&B outlet — restaurant, pool bar, banquet.
